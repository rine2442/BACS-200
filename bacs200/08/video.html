Motivating Perforance

I made this video to let you know the best ways to motivate your employees and their performance in the workplace.

  • Background The five steps in Maslow's Hierarcy of needs are the biggest part of motivation. These five steps are physiological,safety, love and belonging, esteem and lastly self-actualization.
  • How to Motivate Understand that everyone is different and you can not motivate every employee in the same way. A way to understand what your employees need you can have a one-on-one meeting with them to understand how they are motivated and what they are fueled by.
  • Incentives Incentives are a great motivating factor in the world of business. Yes, money can be the biggest motivater but most people are motivated by much simpler things such as awards or family.
